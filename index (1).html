About the Book

A voyage that changes everything

Set against the vast and restless sea, this is a story about what it means to leave — and what it truly means to arrive. With each mile traveled south, the past becomes both anchor and compass.

Part adventure, part meditation on loss and belonging, What Was Carried, What Was Lost asks: when you can only take so much, what do you choose?

About Irene Lee

Irene Lee is a young writer with a passion for adventure stories and the ocean. She wrote What Was Carried, What Was Lost as an exploration of courage, memory, and the journeys that define us.

This is her first published work — and the beginning of many voyages to come.
