About the Book

Based on a true story

While the Korean War rages, twelve-year-old Shin Hwajae is forced to flee North Korea after a violent encounter with soldiers, leaving her family fractured and her future uncertain. Separated from her father and brother, she must navigate a perilous journey across islands and open sea with the family members she has left, hoping to reunite and desperate to survive.

Based on the true story of Irene's grandmother's escape, What Was Carried, What Was Lost is a memoir of loss, sacrifice, and the cost of survival.

About Irene Lee

Irene Lee is a young writer with a passion for adventure stories and the ocean. She wrote What Was Carried, What Was Lost as an exploration of courage, memory, and the journeys that define us.

This is her first published work — and the beginning of many voyages to come.

— Irene Lee
